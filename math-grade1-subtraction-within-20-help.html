Subtraction (within 20)

In this guide, you'll learn what subtraction means, how to count back, use related addition facts, and solve take-away word problems with numbers up to 20.

⏱ ~5 min read 📝 Jump to Worksheet →

Section 1

What is Subtraction?

Subtraction means taking away from a group. When we subtract, the answer (called the difference) is always smaller than what we started with.

The number we start with is the minuend. The number we take away is the subtrahend. The symbol for subtraction is −.

minuend − subtrahend = difference
e.g.  9 − 3 = 6

Section 2

Subtraction Strategies

  1. 1
    Cross Out: Draw dots for the big number, cross out the ones being taken away. Count what's left.
  2. 2
    Count Back: Start at the big number and count backwards. 10 − 3 → start at 10, count back "9, 8, 7". Answer: 7.
  3. 3
    Think Addition: If you know 6 + 5 = 11, then 11 − 5 = 6. Use what you know!
  4. 4
    Subtract 0 or all: Any number minus 0 = itself. Any number minus itself = 0. e.g., 8 − 0 = 8 and 8 − 8 = 0.

Section 3

Worked Examples

Example 1 — Count back
What is 13 − 4?
Start at 13, count back 4 steps
12, 11, 10, 9
✅ Answer: 9
Example 2 — Think addition
What is 15 − 7?
Think: 7 + ? = 15
7 + 8 = 15, so 15 − 7 = 8
✅ Answer: 8
Example 3 — Subtract to find the missing number
12 − ___ = 5. What is the missing number?
Think: 12 − ? = 5
Count back from 12 to 5: that's 7 steps → 12 − 7 = 5
✅ Answer: 7
Example 4 — Related facts
If 9 + 6 = 15, write two subtraction facts from this.
15 − 6 = 9 take away the second addend
15 − 9 = 6 take away the first addend
✅ Answer: 15 − 6 = 9 & 15 − 9 = 6
Example 5 — Word problem
There are 16 birds on a branch. 8 fly away. How many are left?
16 − 8 = ?
8 + 8 = 16 (doubles!), so 16 − 8 = 8
✅ Answer: 8 birds

Section 4

Watch Out! ⚠️

Common Mistakes to Avoid

  • ❌ Subtracting in the wrong order: Thinking 3 − 8 is the same as 8 − 3
    ✅ Subtraction is NOT reversible: 8 − 3 = 5, but 3 − 8 is not possible (in Grade 1). Always take the smaller from the bigger.
  • ❌ Counting back from the wrong starting point: For 11 − 4, counting "11, 10, 9, 8" and stopping there to get 8 (only counted 3 steps)
    ✅ Count back 4 full steps: "10, 9, 8, 7". The answer is 7.
  • ❌ Confusing subtraction with addition: Writing 9 − 3 = 12
    ✅ The answer to a subtraction is always LESS than the starting number: 9 − 3 = 6.

Section 5

Quick Reference

Subtraction symbol
− means "minus", "take away", or "less"
Difference
The answer to a subtraction problem.
Count Back
Start at the big number, count backwards by the smaller.
Think Addition
If a + b = c, then c − a = b and c − b = a.
n − 0 = n
Subtracting 0 from a number leaves it unchanged.
n − n = 0
Subtracting a number from itself always gives 0.
